Sangrah'18

About Sangrah'18

Sangrah’18, the 16 th Annual ISTE State Student Convention of the ISTE Kerala Section was successfully hosted by Government College of Engineering, Kannur from Feb 16 to 18, 2018. A technical symposium along with Open House and LPSC Pearl Jubilee Celebrations from ISRO, the 3 day convention saw intense participation of students and public from all over Kerala. The title sponsor was Byju’s The Learning App. The convention was cosponsored by KSCSTE, and was powered by ISRO and MSME.

Open House

About Open House

The Open House held over a span of 3 days at GCE Kannur was a grand success, with participation of over 3000 people from all walks of life. A whole set of labs, facilities and projects within our walls were made available for all. With the help of useful props, the students of GCE Kannur explained the machinery and the projects on display to the keen public, comprising of high school, higher secondary and polytechnic students, technocrats as well as to the curious common man.

LPSC PEARL JUBILEE CELEBRATIONS

About LPSC PEARL JUBILEE CELEBRATIONS

In association with the LPSC Pearl Jubilee Celebrations, project expo and talks were also organized by the Indian Space Research Organization. An expert talk on Space & Technology was held on 18th February 2018. The talk was handled by Mr. Kiran Mohan, scientist, ISRO. Through the talk, he introduced the students to space science, as well as to the technology that has enabled humanity to explore beyond our planet. The audience was enraptured by the explanations on the basics of rocket flight, evolution of rocketry, sounding rockets and operational launch vehicles. The session saw a participation of over 100 students from different colleges and concluded with an interactive session in which students cleared their doubts.

COMPETITIONS

About COMPETITIONS

Sangrah’18 saw an astounding participation of over 1400 students from over 47 colleges across Kerala. A total of 24 competitive events were held, classified under general, robotics and department-wise categories.

SANGRAH RESONANCE - EXPERT TALK SERIES

About SANGRAH RESONANCE - EXPERT TALK SERIES

An expert talk series was conducted under the banner Sangrah RESONANCE, in which talks were delivered by 5 exciting speakers with specializations in different areas of technology.

WORKSHOPS

About WORKSHOPS

Ten workshops were conducted as part of Sangrah’18, of which four workshops were conducted in the weeks prior to the convention.
